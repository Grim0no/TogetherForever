Vow of Eternal Devotion: A Love That Grows

This project is not just a gift; it is a promise—one written not on paper, but in the stars, carved into time itself. A love that does not wane, does not fade, does not break, no matter the storms we face. If we fall, let us rise. If we falter, let us find each other again, always.

Every word here is a monument to us, every page a love letter that will endure beyond lifetimes. This space, crafted with my hands, my heart, my frustation and my soul, will remain as long as the internet stands— a sanctuary where anyone who stumbles upon it can bear witness to the depth of my devotion.

But this is more than just a monument frozen in time; it is a living creation, just like my love for you. Though it is far from perfect, I will continue to build upon it, just as I continue to build upon my love for you. Every update, every new addition, is a reflection of that growth. A testament that love, like art, is never truly finished.

Throughout history, the greatest loves have been immortalized in paintings, letters, and statues. and although all those are on my list, I think a digital testament fits quite well, a love story etched into the infinite expanse of the web. A place where our love is not just remembered, but experienced. A love immortal, unwavering, and infinite.
